About Jomi Joma BD

Empowering citizens across the Rangpur Division with standard educational documentation blueprints concerning complex land laws.

Our Primary Objective

Property ownership tracking in Bangladesh historically presents layers of administrative terminology (CS, SA, RS Khatians, Dag allocations, and Mutation forms). Our completely static repository simplifies data structures to prevent misinterpretations and optimize compliance workflows.

Architecture Excellence

Built completely void of complex heavy frameworks, this application records zero user tracking analytics to retain absolute maximum performance speeds over distributed edge channels like Cloudflare Pages networks.
