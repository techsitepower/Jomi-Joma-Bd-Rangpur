CS (Cadastral Survey)

The primary basis of land data recorded under the British administration between 1888 and 1940. Critical for identifying ancestral roots.

SA (State Acquisition Survey)

Conducted in 1950 following the abolition of the Zamindari system. Forms the backbone of intermediate title histories.

RS (Revisional Survey)

Modernized record matching executed to resolve ongoing map disputes between state allocations and local settlements.

BS (Bangladesh Survey)

The modern-era continuous digital mapping standard across updated administrative blocks in independent Bangladesh.

Namjari (Mutation)

The vital operational methodology required to legally substitute an operational asset holder's ledger entry post purchase or transfer.
